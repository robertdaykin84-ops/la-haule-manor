Welcome to La Haule Manor

Nestled on the shores of St Aubin's Bay since 1796, La Haule Manor is a distinguished Georgian manor that has been lovingly restored to offer five-star luxury in one of Jersey's most coveted locations. With panoramic views stretching across the bay to Elizabeth Castle, every stay is steeped in history and quiet grandeur.

Our dedicated team provides the kind of personal, attentive service that transforms a hotel stay into something truly memorable. Whether you're strolling the harbour at St Aubin village just moments from our door, enjoying a sundowner on the terrace, or simply taking in the ever-changing tides from your room, La Haule Manor is a place to pause, breathe, and feel at home.

Explore Jersey

From rugged coastal paths to centuries of history, Jersey offers a world of discovery just moments from our door.

Beaches & Coast

St Brelade's Bay and Beauport are minutes away. Walk the coastal paths, discover hidden coves, or simply sink your toes into pristine golden sand.

History & Heritage

Elizabeth Castle stands guard across the bay. Explore Mont Orgueil, the Jersey War Tunnels, and centuries of fascinating island history.

Food & Drink

St Aubin's harbourside is a haven for food lovers. Award-winning seafood restaurants, artisan cafes, and Jersey's renowned local produce await.

Adventure & Nature

Kayak around the coastline, cycle the Green Lanes, or spot puffins at Les Minquiers. Jersey's compact size means adventure is never far away.
