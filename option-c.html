Your Story Begins Here

Step through our doors and feel the warmth of a true Jersey welcome. A chilled glass of bubbly awaits you on the sun-dappled terrace, where the sweep of St Aubin's Bay unfolds before you like a painting come to life.

Since 1796, these Georgian walls have sheltered travellers, dreamers, and those simply seeking a little beauty in their days. Every corner holds a story — from the grand hallway with its original flagstones to the intimate lounges where afternoon light pours through tall sash windows.

As a proud 5-star rated hotel, we believe luxury is not about formality. It is about remembering your name, knowing your favourite room, and making you feel as though you have come home.

Chapter Five

Your Jersey Adventure

Jersey is a small island with an enormous heart. Here are a few of our favourite things to share with our guests.

Beaches & Bays

St Brelade's Bay is a five-minute stroll. Beauport — the secret beach — is a ten-minute drive through country lanes. Our concierge knows every hidden cove.

Concierge tip: Ask us about the low-tide walk to Elizabeth Castle

Castles & History

Mont Orgueil Castle stands guard over Gorey Harbour, and the Jersey War Tunnels tell a powerful story. Elizabeth Castle sits just across the bay from us — watch it appear and vanish with the tides.

Elizabeth Castle: 2 miles, visible from the terrace

Food Trail

From the Fish Market in St Helier to farm shops selling Jersey Royals straight from the earth, this island is a food lover's paradise. We are happy to book our favourite restaurants for you.

Concierge tip: Try the oysters at the harbour — 10 mins away

Coastal Walks

The north coast cliff path is one of the finest walks in the British Isles. Closer to home, the promenade from La Haule to St Helier follows the curve of the bay — perfect for a morning stroll or sunset wander.

Promenade walk starts right outside our door
