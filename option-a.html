Welcome

A Georgian Treasure on St Aubin's Bay

Perched above the golden sweep of St Aubin’s Bay, La Haule Manor has been welcoming guests to Jersey since 1796. Our handsome Georgian manor blends timeless elegance with contemporary comfort, earning a coveted 5-star rating from Visit Jersey.

Every stay is personal here. From the moment you arrive, our small, dedicated team ensures you feel at home — whether you’re unwinding by our heated outdoor pool with panoramic bay views, savouring a locally sourced breakfast, or simply breathing in the sea air from your private balcony.

With just 17 individually designed rooms, La Haule Manor offers the intimacy of a boutique retreat with the service standards of a luxury hotel. This is Jersey as it should be experienced — unhurried, beautiful, and distinctly personal.

Celebrations

Your Dream Wedding

Imagine exchanging vows with the sun setting over St Aubin’s Bay, Elizabeth Castle silhouetted against a golden sky. At La Haule Manor, your wedding is exclusively yours — the entire manor, gardens, and our devoted team.

From intimate ceremonies for 20 to grand celebrations for up to 120 guests, our tailored wedding packages include bespoke menus crafted with the finest Jersey produce, a dedicated wedding coordinator, and 17 individually styled rooms for your party.

Dining

Taste Jersey

From freshly caught seafood to island-grown produce, every meal celebrates Jersey’s rich culinary heritage.

Start Your Day

Wake to the aroma of freshly brewed coffee and a breakfast spread that has earned its own following. Our award-winning full English features free-range eggs from a local farm, Jersey Royal potatoes, and artisan bread baked fresh each morning.

Prefer something lighter? Choose from smoked salmon with cream cheese, seasonal fruit, homemade granola with Jersey cream, or fluffy pancakes with maple syrup and crispy bacon.

Included with every stay

Unwind

As the sun dips behind Elizabeth Castle, retreat to our lounge bar for handcrafted cocktails and locally inspired drinks. Our bartenders mix classic favourites alongside Jersey specials — try the La Haule Sundowner with local apple brandy and fresh citrus.

Pair your drink with a selection of bar bites: Jersey crab croquettes, truffle fries, or a sharing board of island cheeses and charcuterie. Open daily from 11am to late.

Afternoon Tea

A Jersey tradition reimagined. Settle into our sun-drenched lounge or the garden terrace for a three-tier afternoon tea featuring delicate finger sandwiches, freshly baked scones with Jersey clotted cream and local jam, and an exquisite selection of patisserie.

Upgrade to our Champagne Tea for that special celebration. Available Thursday to Sunday, booking recommended.

Explore

Discover Jersey

From pristine beaches to ancient castles, Jersey packs a world of experiences into a beautiful island just nine miles by five.

Beaches

Jersey boasts some of Europe’s finest beaches. St Brelade’s Bay is a 5-minute drive, while the dramatic north coast cliffs offer wilder shores and rock pools.

St Brelade’s Bay — 5 min drive

History

Walk across the causeway to Elizabeth Castle, explore the Jersey War Tunnels, or discover 6,000 years of history at La Hougue Bie — one of Europe’s finest passage graves.

Elizabeth Castle — visible from hotel

Nature

Spot puffins at Les Écrehous, stroll through the stunning Durrell Wildlife Conservation Trust, or explore 50 miles of coastal paths with wildflowers and sea views.

Durrell Wildlife — 15 min drive

Food & Drink

Jersey is a foodie island. Visit the Central Market, sample Jersey Royals and fresh oysters at harbourside restaurants, or tour La Mare Wine Estate.

Central Market — 10 min drive

Adventure

Kayak around the coastline, try coasteering, paddleboard at sunset, or hire bikes to explore quiet green lanes. Jersey’s compact size makes everything accessible.

Bike hire available nearby

Family

Jersey Zoo, aMaizin! Adventure Park, and miles of safe sandy beaches make the island a family paradise. Our heated pool is a firm favourite with little ones too.

Jersey Zoo — 15 min drive
